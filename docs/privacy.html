Privacy Policy

Last Updated: August 2026

1. Data Collection

VC Guardian Bot collects only the minimum information required to provide its functionality:

  • Voice channel member counts
  • Voice channel activity status
  • Voice activity streak information
  • Temporary warning states used for notifications

This information is stored locally by the bot and is used exclusively for voice channel tracking features.

2. Data We Do Not Collect

The bot does not collect:

  • Message content
  • Direct messages
  • Email addresses
  • Passwords or login credentials
  • IP addresses
  • Personal information outside of Discord identifiers required for operation

3. How Data Is Used

Collected information is used solely to:

  • Track voice channel activity streaks
  • Generate voice channel statistics
  • Send voice channel activity alerts
  • Maintain bot functionality

4. Data Storage

  • Data is stored locally on the machine hosting the bot.
  • No external database is required by default.
  • No collected data is sold or shared with third parties.

5. User Rights

Users may request data resets or removal of stored statistics where technically possible. Requests should be directed to the server administrator or bot owner.

6. Contact

For questions regarding this Privacy Policy, please contact the bot owner through Discord.
